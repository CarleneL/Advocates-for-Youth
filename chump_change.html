The Chump Change Project

Since February 2016, the Chump Change Project has raised over $200.00 in change and had the ability to create 10 care packages thus far. Each care package is separated by men and women needs.

200

How it Works

Each care package is delivered with a hot meal (varies) to the individual. In hopes to alleviate hunger in the community and provide essential needs to individual, the Chump Change Project will be the first stepping stone to doing the right thing.

By the end of the year, Advocates for Young People is striving to have created at least 300 care packages with the intention to double in the years to come. Have something you want to add to the care package? Find out how today!
